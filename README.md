### How to run:
1. **Download** the latest `.zip` or `.tar.gz` archive for your operating system.
2. **Extract** the contents to a folder of your choice.
3. **Run** the executable:
   * **Windows:** Double-click `beatemup.exe`.
   * **Linux:** Execute `./beatemup_game` via terminal.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> All assets (graphics, levels, fonts) are included in the archive. Please do not move the executable out of its folder, as it relies on relative paths to load game data.
